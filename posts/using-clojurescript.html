2 Working with HTML

6 Reagent

https://purelyfunctional.tv/guide/reagent/ Reagent has its own Atoms. They're just like regular Clojure Atoms in every way except they do one extra thing: they keep track of Reagent components who have derefed them. Every time those Atoms change values, the component will be re-rendered.
